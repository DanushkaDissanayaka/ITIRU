Keynote Abstract

Professor Hooman Samani

Title:

Integration for Affective Human-Robot Interaction

Abstract:

In this talk, the integration of emotions in cognitive robotics will be discussed as a multidisciplinary research field utilizing fundamentals concepts from robotics, artificial intelligence, philosophy, psychology, biology, anthropology, neuroscience, social science, computer science and engineering. Proposed approach provides a system allowing the development of functionalities based on multidisciplinary research yielding a new form of affective interaction between robots and humans.
