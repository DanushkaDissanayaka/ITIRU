Camera Ready Paper Instructions

Preparing Camera Ready Paper

  • Please edit your paper by incorporating all reviewers' comments and use IEEE conference paper format. Please note that organizers reserve the right to reject your paper, if you don't modify your paper accordingly.
  • The final camera-ready paper submission must be limited to 6 pages.
  • It is strictly required to IEEE format and referencing style. The latest IEEE template could be found at https://www.ieee.org/conferences/publishing/templates.html. The papers which do not follow IEEE template will not be accepted by IEEE Xplore and cannot be published.
  • Please DO NOT include page numbers in the final manuscript submission.
  • The appropriate copyright clearance code notice is to appear on the bottom, left-hand side of the first page of the paper, as follows:
    • For papers in which all authors are employed by the US government, the copyright notice is: U.S. Government work not protected by U.S. copyright
    • For papers in which all authors are employed by a Crown government (UK, Canada, and Australia), the copyright notice is: 978-1-5386-4417-1/18/$31.00 ©2018 Crown
    • For papers in which all authors are employed by the European Union, the copyright notice is: 978-1-5386-4417-1/18/$31.00 ©2018 European Union
    • For all other papers the copyright notice is: 978-1-5386-4417-1/18/$31.00 ©2018 IEEE

Preparing the paper IEEE PDF compliant usng IEEE PDF eXpress

  • Before submit the paper, authors are instructed to prepare the paper IEEE PDF compliant.
    1. Add the copyright notice to the bottom of the bottom, left-hand side of the first page of the paper
    2. Proofread your source document thoroughly to confirm that it will require no revision.
  • Follow the following instructions to prepare IEEE compliant PDF version of your accepted paper, this is compulsory for all accepted papers.
  • Log in to the IEEE PDF eXpress TM site using http://www.pdf-express.org/

      • Password
    3. Continue to enter information as prompted. An Online confirmation will be displayed and an email confirmation will be sent verifying your account setup. IEEE Copyright Form

  • Authors of accepted paper(s) must submit completed copyright form(s) along with the final manuscript submission. Papers submitted without copyright form will not be published.
  • Fill your copyright form as follows
    1. Write your paper title at
        TITLE OF PAPER/ARTICLE/REPORT, INCLUDING ALL CONTENT IN ANY FORM, FORMAT, OR MEDIA (hereinafter, “the Work”):
    2. Write list of authors at
        COMPLETE LIST OF AUTHORS:
    3. Write the conference title at
        IEEE PUBLICATION TITLE (Journal, Magazine, Conference, Book): 2019 4th International Conference on Information Technology Research (ICITR) (49409) 4. Place your signature and date.
