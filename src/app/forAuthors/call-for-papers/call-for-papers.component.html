Call for Papers

The ICITR 2019 is soliciting technical papers for oral presentations at its 4th International Conference on Information Technology Research. It provides all researches the presenters and participants with a great opportunity to present and discuss the latest accomplishments, challenges, and future directions in the field and allied fields. The authors are invited to submit their full papers to ICITR2019. The submissions will be peer-reviewed for originality and scientific quality.
